Aba Tech Innovators Club

Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Aba Tech Innovators Club

The name represents a growing community of developers, entrepreneurs, and technology enthusiasts in Aba who collaborate on projects, share knowledge, and inspire innovation.

Optional Domain: abatechinnovators.org

Site Purpose

The website serves as a digital hub for technology enthusiasts in Aba by providing information about the club, upcoming events, coding workshops, hackathons, tutorials, mentorship opportunities, and member projects.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Aba Tech Innovators Club?
  • When is the next hackathon or coding workshop?
  • Where can I find tutorials and mentorship opportunities?
  • What projects have club members developed?
